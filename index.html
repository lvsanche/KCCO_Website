Project CSMap

Problem

Don't you hate it when google maps send you the wrong way to class on the first day? Or having no route to follow on maps.ucsd.edu? Well now with project CSMaps these troubles will no longer be. Our project will be a crowd source map, in which everyone can find the best way to get to class, add their own favorite routes, and even rate them.
